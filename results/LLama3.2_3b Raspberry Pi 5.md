7. **Causality**: The speed of light implies causality, meaning that cause precedes effect.
8. **Quantum mechanics**: The speed of light plays a crucial role in quantum mechanics, particularly in the study

In summary, the speed of light is an fundamental constant in physics that represents the maximum speed at which
all energy, matter, and information can travel in a vacuum. Its measurement has led to significant advances in our
understanding of the universe, from special relativity to quantum mechanics.
